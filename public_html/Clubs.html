Computer Science Club

We provide an educational and fun outlet for students to pursue their interest in computer science outside the classroom. Our club sponsors a number of guest speakers and numerous events throughout the year, including programming contests and various shows. We are actively involved in the open source movement and a proud supporter of the Linux operating system. We encourage all majors to join.

Day of the week and time you hold your club meetings:

Every other Wednesdays from 7p.m. to 9p.m. at RB 328

Women in Computing Club

Joining this club is a great way to involve and engage women into the computer science field. We provide valuable networking between Siena students and successful career professionals by encouraging attendance in various computer science conferences. Not only do we interact with college students and professionals, but we also introduce the field of computers and technology to students of the primary school and high school age. We increase technological knowledge in a fun and inclusive environment.

Day of the week and time you hold your club meetings:

Wednesdays during free period once a month
